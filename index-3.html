About Our Agency

Sybex Support Services is a premier provider of all kinds of support services to cater your business needs. We are a single source company, who you can trust to provide support services such as recruitment/staffing needs, Security Guard/Protection services and Cleaning/Janitorial services. Sybex can cater all types of businesses, whether small or medium to large and governments, we have done it all. Thanks to our perfect business model, which allows us to work with your internal team to specifically understand your needs and develop customized plans and solutions to meet your goals effectively and efficiently, saving you both time and money.
